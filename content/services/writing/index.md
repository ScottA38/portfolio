## The Process

_My_ process consists of:

- I write you great content
- It greases [the marketing funnel](https://www.smartinsights.com/digital-marketing-strategy/race-a-practical-framework-to-improve-your-digital-marketing/). It increases [dwell time](https://www.semrush.com/blog/dwell-time/).
- You get more people clicking through and [exploring your services or products](/services).
- You get customers.
- You become wealthy, happy and content.
<!--We take a holistically-minded approach to improving consumer messaging and getting potential customers to identify with your offerring.-->

_Hmm, I might_ have simplified things a little bit.

Writing <u>good content</u> which _truly enacts a sense of consumer trust_ requires a <u>steely determination</u>, and as a _sensitive_, _nuanced_ approach to **incorporating the many diverse aspects of your brand offerring** that are _unique_, and that can _benefit others_.

Crucially then, _orchestrating such a story_ requires an **abundance of _human tactility_**.
